Jessica Frost's Biography

I am a political science major interested in international law and human rights law. I am minoring in information management and technology. At Syracuse University I have been Secretary of Kappa Kappa Gamma, a TA for a First-Year SEM class, and volunteered at RISE Syracuse. Last summer, I interned for Governor Andrew Cuomo and then for New York State Senator Jeremy Cooney this past fall. I have earned a Dean’s List recognition every semester so far.
