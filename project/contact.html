About

Rachel Frost, Esq.

Editor-in-Chief

Rachel Frost, Esq. is a double Seton Hall University alumna and recently admitted attorney.

Rachel attended Seton Hall University and graduated in May 2017 with a B.S in Business Administration: Marketing and Legal Studies in Business. Rachel graduated as summa cum laude and valedictorian from the Stillman School of Business with a cumulative 4.0 GPA.

Picture source link

Past experience includes:

  • Law clerk and summer associate at Greenbaum Rowe Smith & Davis, LLP.
  • Legal extern to the Section Chief of Data Privacy and Cybersecurity in the New Jersey Office of the Attorney General.
  • Judicial extern for the Hon. Esther Salas, U.S District Court for the District of New Jersey.
  • In-house corporate counsel intern at Prudential Financial Inc.

Rachel attended law school at Seton Hall Law and graduated in May 2020. Currently, Rachel works at the law firm Greenbaum Rowe Smith & Davis, LLP. in their litigation department. Rachel focuses her practice in commercial litigation.

Since a young age, Rachel had seemingly separate passions for fashion, business, and law. As she got older, Rachel became inspired by the legal world. Rachel realized how the fashion and business industries, which she loved to learn from, were heavily affected by the legal world.
