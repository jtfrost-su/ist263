Mission Statement

The Legal Runway exists to marry the areas of business, fashion, and law.

Our Chief Principles

TLR aspires to make readers more informed every day, whether your cup of tea is fashion, business, law- or all three! Our goal is to provide content to our readers of various professional backgrounds with insights into the interplay of business, fashion, and law.

The principles that TLR follow are:

  1. Commitment- Responsibility to consistently achieve the goals we set.
  2. Validity- Thoroughly researched content offered with analysis and insight.
  3. Independence- Objective content that is free from all forms of bias, agenda, and political partisanship.

Our Ethics Standards

TLR exists to objectively serve our readers. We value our stringent ethics standards. We strive to exceed current industry standards as it relates to fashion, business, and law to ensure that our content is accurate, complete, and insightful.
  1. Credibility- We will always give credit to any outside material referenced in our content.
  2. Objectivity- We will always report with a lack of bias and subjective analysis. We will instead offer our analysis on new findings.
  3. Reliability- We will strive to offer recent, updated reports as well as any due updates/reaffirmations on previous reporting to ensure reliability of the content.
*Disclaimer- The content provided on this website shall not, and does not serve the role to, authorize legal advice. Rather, all content and sources referenced on this website serve the role to inform the readers of objective analyses of the world of fashion and business as it relates to law.
